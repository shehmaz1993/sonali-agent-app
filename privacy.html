Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 12, 2026

The Sonali Agent App ("we", "our", or "the App") is dedicated to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your Google user data, specifically regarding authentication and Google Calendar integration.

1. Data Accessed & Scopes

Our App requests access to the following data via Google OAuth:

  • Google Identity: Your email address and basic profile info to verify your identity as an authorized agent.
  • Google Calendar API: Specifically to create, view, and edit calendar events related to client schedules.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data accessed strictly for the following functional purposes:

  • Authentication: To ensure only authorized agents of Sonali Life Insurance Co. Ltd. can access the internal portal.
  • Scheduling: To allow agents to set appointments with clients. The App will insert these meetings into your Google Calendar so you can manage your professional schedule efficiently.
  • Non-Intrusion: We do not read your personal or private calendar events that were not created by this App.

3. Data Sharing & Disclosure

Third-Party Sharing: We do not share, sell, or trade your Google user data with any third parties. Your calendar data and email information are never shared with outside advertisers or data brokers.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Security: All communication between the App and Google Servers is encrypted via SSL/TLS.
  • Storage: We do not store your Google password. Profile and calendar data are accessed in real-time via Google’s secure APIs.

5. Data Deletion & Revoking Access

You may revoke the App's access to your Google account at any time through your Google Security Settings. To request that we delete any associated account records from our system, please contact us at the email below.
