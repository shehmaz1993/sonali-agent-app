Sonali Agent App

The Sonali Agent App is designed for Sonali Life Insurance agents to efficiently manage and monitor their business activities. Through this application, agents can access their monthly, quarterly, and yearly premium reports, as well as deferred renewal information. In addition, the app provides comprehensive insights into individual performance, commission details, overall company performance, total collections, commission schedules, and provident fund information. Agents can also view due policies, lapsed policies, claims, and maturity details, along with the ability to perform lead entry. Furthermore, the app enables agents to schedule and manage appointments with clients for various policy-related services, ensuring better customer engagement and service delivery.
